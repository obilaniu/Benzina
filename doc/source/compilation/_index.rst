Compile Benzina
===============

Requirements for compilation:

* `Python <https://www.python.org>`_ 3.5 or later
* `Meson <https://mesonbuild.com>`_ 0.49 or later
* `Ninja <https://github.com/ninja-build/ninja>`_
* `Nauka <https://github.com/obilaniu/Nauka>`_ 0.0.11 or later
* `Numpy <https://www.numpy.org>`_ 1.10 or later

Requirements to use the PyTorch interface:

* `PyTorch <https://pytorch.org>`_ 0.4.1 or later
